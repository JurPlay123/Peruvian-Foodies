Mazamorra
Morada

The mazamorra morada is one of the best Peruvian desserts.

Ingredients (8 to 12 servings)

  • 1/2 kg of purple corn (maíz morado)
  • 1/2 pineapple peel
  • 1 apple
  • 1 quince
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 5 cloves
  • 1/2 teaspoon orange zest
  • 1/2 cup of morello cherries
  • 1/2 cup of bones
  • 1/2 cup sour cherries
  • 1/2 pineapple (preferably golden)
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 5 teaspoons sweet potato flour
  • Cinnamon powder
  • 2 litres of water

Preparation (90 minutes)


  1. Shuck the purple corn.

  2. Place the water in a pot.

  3. Add the purple corn, pineapple peel, apple, quince, cinnamon, cloves and orange zest. Stir.

  4. Boil for at least 40 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  5. Strain the mixture into one pot and boil only the liquid in another pot.

  6. Reserve one cup of the liquid for the end.

  7. Add the morello cherries, the pimentos, the sour cherries, the pineapple and the sugar. Keep stirring.

  8. Boil for about 20 minutes over medium heat.

  9. Dissolve the sweet potato flour in the reserved liquid.

  10. Add the dissolved sweet potato flour to the pot.

  11. Stir until the ingredients are thickened.

  12. Sirve y espolvorea la canela en polvo.
