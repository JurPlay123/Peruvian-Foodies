Chicha
Morada

The chicha morada,is a Peruvian drink made from a Peruvian vegetable.

Ingredients (3 litres)

  • 1 kilo of purple corn (Maíz Morado)
  • 1 pineapple (peels and leftover fruit)
  • 1/4 kilo of quince
  • 3 litres of water
  • 10 grams of whole cinnamon
  • 8 cloves
  • Sugar
  • 3 lemons

Preparation (60 to 180 minutes)


  1. Shuck the purple corn.

  2. Place the purple corn in a pot.

  3. Add the pineapple peel, quince and water.

  4. Take it to the fire.

  5. Add the cinnamon and cloves.

  6. Let them boil for 45 min.

  7. Leave to cool for a couple of hours.

  8. Strain it.

  9. Sweeten to taste.

  10. Add lemon juice. Stir.

  11. Pour it.

Tips:


  • The peel and parts of the pineapple are not eaten. Reserve the pulp for eating.

  • If you want to store the chicha morada, do not add sugar or lemon, and refrigerate or freeze it tightly covered.
