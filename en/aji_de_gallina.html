Ají de
Gallina

The Ají de Gallina, is one of the best Peruvian dishes. Here is the recipe.

Ingredients (8 servings)

  • 12 slices of sliced bread
  • 1 chicken in 8 pieces
  • 2 1/2 cups chopped onion
  • 3 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon chopped garlic
  • 2 tablespoons ground aji amarillo pepper
  • 150 grams grated Parmesan cheese
  • 200 grams chopped walnuts
  • 8 cooked yellow potatoes, peeled and sliced
  • 4 boiled eggs, sliced
  • Salt
  • 8 botija olives* (Optional)

Preparation (80 minutes)


  1. Cook the chicken in a pot with 3 litres of water and a pinch of salt for 50 minutes, or until tender. Strain, shred the chicken and soak the bread in the stock for the next 15 minutes. Strain and reserve the bread and 2 cups of stock separately.

  2. Heat the oil in a saucepan over medium heat and brown the onion for 3 minutes. From there, add the garlic, and cook until 3 minutes have passed. Add the ground chilli and mix. Season with salt.

  3. Pour in the strained bread with the stock, Parmesan cheese and walnuts. Cook these ingredients, stirring until slightly thickened. Stir in the shredded chicken, mix and simmer over low heat for an additional 5 minutes. Check seasoning.

  4. Serve the chilli with the potatoes, eggs and olives*.
